About Me & What I Do

Hello all, I’m Tom and I specialise in building fast, scalable, responsive front-ends (HTML / CSS / JS) and implementing these front-end templates into fully bespoke WordPress themes (I also specialise in and know my way around a lot of other CMS theming such as Shopify, Big Cartel, Jekyll, Ghost etc.)

There's nothing I love more than developing responsive websites, making them as performant as I can and testing in as many browsers / devices I have at my disposal, I then move on to making the content management a breeze. In short: I make sure as many users as possible will have an awesome user experience browsing the site.

If you are interested in working with me on a project, I am available for freelance work so don’t hesitate to get in touch with me so we can get the ball rolling.
